BreakOut

The famous, classic, brick-breaking game.

How to Play?

It's simple:

  • Control the paddle with the keyboard left and right arrow keys, or the mouse pointer.
  • Try to bounce the ball back to break all bricks.
  • Catch or avoid any items that fall in your way.
  • Don't let the ball hit the bottom or you will lose your lives.

Hints

  • Watch out! The ball gets faster as it hits the bricks.
  • Gray bricks never break they are metallic.
  • You can increase your score with some items.
  • Have Fun!
GAME OVER!
